About

AI-Powered Environmental Cleanliness Analysis

EcoScope is an AI-powered environmental analysis platform designed to evaluate cleanliness from uploaded images.

Using deep learning and computer vision techniques, EcoScope classifies environments as either Clean or Dirty, helping users better understand and monitor environmental conditions.

Built using TensorFlow and Flask, the platform provides a simple and accessible interface for image-based cleanliness assessment.

Our goal is to encourage environmental awareness through practical AI tools that are easy to use and accessible to everyone.
